# 📊 Bank Marketing Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Bank Marketing Dashboard developed 
in Microsoft Excel using the Bank Marketing dataset. The dashboard provides 
insights into customer demographics, marketing campaign performance, and 
deposit subscription trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer demographics
- Monitor deposit subscription performance
- Identify customer behavior across age groups, education, and marital status
- Understand monthly contact trends
- Create an interactive dashboard for business reporting

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset contains customer information collected during a bank marketing campaign.

**Features include:**
- Age
- Job
- Marital Status
- Education
- Balance
- Contact Type
- Loan
- Housing Loan
- Campaign
- Previous Contact
- Deposit Subscription

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
The following preprocessing steps were performed:
- Removed duplicate records
- Checked for missing values
- Converted data into Excel Tables
- Created calculated fields:
  - Age Group
  - Balance Category
  - Campaign Category
  - Previous Contact Category

## 📊 Key Insights
- Most customers belong to the **30–39 age group**
- **Secondary education** customers represent the largest customer segment
- **Married** customers form the majority of the customer base
- Deposit subscriptions show a nearly balanced acceptance rate of **47.38%**
- Customer contacts peaked during the month of **May**
- **Retired and Student** segments show the highest deposit acceptance rates
- **Cellular** contact method performs better than telephone

---

## 🎯 Recommendations
- Focus outreach on Retired and Student segments
- Prioritize cellular contact over telephone calls
- Increase campaign intensity in March, September and December
- Re-target customers who succeeded in previous campaigns
- Develop targeted offers for tertiary-educated customer segments
- Optimize campaigns for the 30-39 age group
